Welcome to Eagleye a one-stop shop application that allows you to cross reference National Parks information with real time weather forecast.

Wondering if you should bring your raincoat? We have you covered! We combined HTML/CSS, Javascript, JQuery, National Parks API, and Weather API working together to gather all park information, weather alerts, and your daily weather forecast - for your adventure!
